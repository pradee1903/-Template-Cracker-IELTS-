What is IELTS?

The International English Language Testing System is, international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. It is jointly managed by the British Council, IDP: IELTS Australia and Cambridge Assessment English, and was established in 1989. students who seek education abroad opt for the test as it is the No.1in the world and is accepted in more than 11,000 universities over the world!.
